MINECRAFT ART

This is the first time I play Minecraft, and it was quite challenging. Thinking of an object to build, I chose the Pokémon franchise because I was amazed at how long it has remain popular and relevant during the rise of games available on the market.

​Specifically, I did a Pokéball because I was thinking about Pokémon GO, a mobile game that I consider to be excel at taking an old concept and make it becomes innovative in term of user experience. It combines physical and virtual spaces in gameplay while being accessible for everyone to download and try out.
