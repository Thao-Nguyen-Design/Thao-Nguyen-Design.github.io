MASS SCHOOL SHOOTING SINCE COLUMBINE

Mass shooting is defined as an incident in which 4 or more victims, not including the suspect, are killed.

There are hundreds of school shootings that have taken place across the U.S, but 11 can be classified as mass shootings.

Here is the list of them, in chronological order.
